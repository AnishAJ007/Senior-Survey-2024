Welcome to the survey website for graduating students of IIT Bombay. This website gathers valuable feedback from the outgoing batch, providing a comprehensive overview of their experiences and outcomes during their time at the institute. The survey is divided into three key sections: Academics, Career and Personal. We have also included some comments about their time and experiences here at IITB at the end of the Miscellaneous section.
